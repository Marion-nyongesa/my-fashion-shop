Delivery Information

We offer free delivery on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, there is a flat shipping fee of $5.

We ship all orders within 24 hours of receiving them. Delivery times vary depending on your location, but you can expect to receive your order within 3-5 business days.

We offer a variety of shipping options, including standard shipping, expedited shipping, and international shipping. You can choose your shipping option at checkout.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your order on the carrier's website.
